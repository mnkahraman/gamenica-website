Overview

GAMENICA Applications And Games Studio ("GAMENICA", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users of our mobile applications and games. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information across all of our apps published on the Apple App Store and Google Play.

Our core principle: We collect only what is necessary for each app to function. We do not sell your personal data to anyone, ever.

By using any GAMENICA application, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Information We Collect

The data collected varies by app. Below is an overview of the categories of data that may be collected across our portfolio.

Data Type When Collected Where Stored
Name / Email / Phone Never —
Device location Only in apps that require it, with your permission Device / forwarded to relevant API
App preferences & saved content Where the feature exists Device only
Device identifiers & IP address Via third-party SDKs (e.g. AdMob) Third-party servers
Crash / diagnostic logs Via Apple / Google platform tools only Apple / Google servers
Analytics Never (we do not integrate analytics SDKs) —

We do not require account registration across any of our apps. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifier directly.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to deliver the core functionality of each app. Specifically:

  • Location data — used in real time to provide location-based features (e.g. maps, weather, nearby content). It is not stored on our servers or used for profiling.
  • Locally stored preferences — used to remember your in-app settings and saved content across sessions.
  • Device identifiers (via AdMob) — used by Google to serve advertisements. See the Advertising section for details and opt-out options.

We do not use any collected information for marketing, profiling, or resale purposes.

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps integrate third-party services to provide their core features. Each third party operates under its own privacy policy.

Apple Maps / MapKit

Used in apps that include mapping or routing features. Apple may process location data in accordance with their privacy policy: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Google Maps / Google APIs

Used in apps that require mapping or places data on Android or cross-platform. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

OpenWeatherMap

Used in weather-related apps to retrieve weather data. Coordinate data may be forwarded to OpenWeatherMap. Their privacy policy: openweathermap.org/privacy-policy.

Cloudflare

Some apps route API requests through Cloudflare infrastructure for security and performance. Cloudflare may log request metadata (IP address, timestamps). Their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Google AdMob

Used for in-app advertising where applicable. See the Advertising section below.

Apple App Store / Google Play

Standard platform diagnostics and crash reports may be collected by Apple and Google through their respective developer tools. These are governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies.

Advertising

Some GAMENICA apps display advertisements served by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA / Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device type, OS version, language, and time zone

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID"
  • EU / EEA / UK: A consent prompt is shown before any personalized ad is served.
Children's apps: Any GAMENICA app designated for children uses non-personalized ads only. No behavioral or interest-based targeting is applied in those apps.

Push Notifications

Some GAMENICA apps may request permission to send push notifications (e.g. alerts, reminders, or in-app updates). Where supported:

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings.
  • We do not use push notifications to serve advertisements.

Children's Privacy

Apps designed for children collect no personal information from players.

COPPA (United States)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our general-audience apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through those apps.

For apps specifically designed for children, we apply the following additional protections:

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No behavioral advertising is shown.
  • No social features, in-app chat, or user-generated content.
  • No links to external websites.
  • Game progress is saved locally on the device only.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through one of our apps, contact us at mnkahraman@gmail.com and we will delete such data promptly.

GDPR-K (European Union / EEA / UK)

For users in the EU, EEA, or UK, we comply with GDPR and applicable children's data protection requirements. We do not process personal data of children under 16 (or the applicable age in each member state) without verifiable parental consent. In practice, our children's apps collect no personal data at all, making this a non-issue in those apps.

Google Play Families Policy

Apps published by GAMENICA in the Google Play Families program comply with Google's Families Policy, including restrictions on data collection, advertising targeting, and in-app purchases.

Apple Kids Category

Apps submitted to the Apple App Store Kids category comply with Apple's guidelines, including advertising restrictions and the requirement that no personal data is collected without parental consent.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restrict processing — request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — object to data processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time (e.g. revoke location access via device Settings).

Since GAMENICA does not maintain a user database, most data can be managed directly on your device. For location or other permissions, adjust them in your device Settings. To remove all locally stored data, uninstall the app. For any remaining request, contact us at mnkahraman@gmail.com. We respond to all privacy requests within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest:

  • All communication between our apps and our backend services uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • API keys and server credentials are stored as encrypted environment secrets and are never embedded in app binaries.
  • We do not log or retain user data on our servers beyond what is required to fulfill a single request.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over third-party networks.

Data Retention

GAMENICA does not operate a user database or persistent backend storage for personal data. All app data (preferences, saved content, game progress) is stored locally on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob and Cloudflare may retain data in accordance with their own retention policies, which are linked in the Third-Party Services section.

Links to Other Sites

Our apps and this website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Apps designed for children do not contain links to external websites.

Continued use of any GAMENICA app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
