Contact Support

For app support, bug reports, privacy questions, or store-related questions, contact GAMENICA by email.

Email: mnkahraman@gmail.com

Please include the app name, your device model, iOS or Android version, and a short description of the issue. Screenshots are helpful when the issue is visual.

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure the latest version of the app is installed from the official store.
  • Restart the app and, if needed, restart the device.
  • Check that sound, notifications, or location permissions are enabled when the app needs them.
  • If the issue continues, email support with the app name and device details.

Privacy Requests

GAMENICA does not operate a user account database for these apps. Most app data is stored locally on your device.

For privacy questions or data requests, email mnkahraman@gmail.com or review the Privacy Policy.
